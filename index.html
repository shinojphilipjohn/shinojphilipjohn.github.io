Customer Churn Prediction

Using historical customer behaviour to predict churn and support proactive retention strategies.
In Progress

This project focuses on understanding why customers leave and how we can predict churn ahead of time. The workflow follows a clear, production-friendly pattern: EDA → feature engineering → model training → evaluation.

I explore relationships between tenure, contract type, payment methods and service usage to uncover the key drivers of churn, and then train ML models to estimate churn risk at the customer level.
