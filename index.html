How I approach projects

Whether it’s churn prediction or payroll automation, I try to keep a consistent structure across projects so they’re easy to understand, extend and review:

  • Start from a clear business question and success metric.
  • Perform targeted EDA to shape the data model and feature set, not just generate charts.
  • Use clean, modular notebooks or scripts with clear stages.
  • Focus on evaluation that reflects business impact, not just a single score.
  • Document assumptions, limitations and next steps for future iterations or deployment.
